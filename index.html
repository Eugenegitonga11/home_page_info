About URO-CARE Nairobi

Nairobi's Specialist Urology & Andrology Center — Serving Kenya's Men's Health Needs Since 2024

URO-CARE is Nairobi's dedicated urology and andrology center, providing specialist urological care to men across Kenya. Our multidisciplinary team of board-certified urologists, andrologists and nephrologists offers comprehensive evaluation and treatment of all urological conditions — from prostate cancer screening and male infertility to kidney stone management and erectile dysfunction. With our on-site ISO-certified laboratory and fully stocked in-house pharmacy, patients receive complete, integrated care under one roof. We serve patients from Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and across East Africa who require specialist urological expertise.

🔬 Urology Laboratory Services in Nairobi

Our on-site laboratory provides PSA testing, semen analysis, testosterone profiling, kidney function panels, urinalysis, STI screening, and over 40 specialized diagnostic tests for men's urological health — with results ready in 24–48 hours without outsourcing.

💊 Urology Pharmacy Nairobi

Our in-house pharmacy is stocked exclusively with urology and andrology medications — prostate treatments, testosterone replacement therapies, fertility supplements, Erectile Dysfunction medications, antibiotic courses and post-operative supplies. Prescriptions filled immediately after your consultation.
